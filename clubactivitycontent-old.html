Club Activities:

The School offers classes on a regular basis to hone the artistic skills of the students. Students work and learn in a well-equipped art room . Workshops and classes are offered in ceramics, fiber, metals/jewellry, painting, drawing, photography, mixed media, books and paper. Our students participate in various Painting Competitions, Rangoli Competition,On the Spot Painting Competition, Poster Competition and many more conducted by various organizations and time and again bring laurels to the school.
