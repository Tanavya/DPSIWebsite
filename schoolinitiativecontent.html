School Programmes
School Initiatives



DPS International takes pride in implementing the best global practices in education. It offers its students the excellence associated with the Delhi Public Schools along with the school programmes designed to address the challenges and needs of adolescence in an ever changing world.

VALUE EDUCATION PROGRAMME

In the Junior School (Nursery - Class V) students are sensitized to human needs and values through a programme "Little Souls". This programme skill fully combines world mythology, inspirational stories, and folk tales, with craft work. The Programme instills in students qualities of citizenship, and teaches them the importance of caring for the environment, their community and the world.

LIFE SKILLS PROGRAMME

The middle and senior school students benefit from a Life Skills Programme dealing with adolescent issues. Students learn how to cope in the real world in the unthreatening environment of the classroom. Regular workshops are held on issues like peer pressure, stress and anger management, AIDS awareness and substance abuse.

COMMUNITY SERVICE PROGRAMME

The motto of the DPS school is 'Service before Self'. Students put in a number of hours of compulsory service as part of the IAYP (Duke of Edinburgh Award). This includes in school and out of school programmes. The buddy system, mentoring of juniors and the Each One Teach One programme are some ways by which students follow the school precepts.

CAREER COUNSELLING SESSIONS

Regular career counselling sessions are conducted by trained professionals to help students and parents take informed decisions. Students are assisted in making subject choices for Grade IX and the 'A' Levels. These career counselling sessions also guide students through the process of applying to colleges in India and abroad.

THE INDIAN EXPERIENCE

Students are provided with opportunities to experience the diversity of India through educational trips and adventure camps to places of interest within the city and around India. The Fine Arts Department takes students on a journey of India through the folk and classical songs and dances even as students learn Warli, Madhubani and other folk and classical painting styles in their art classes.

INTERNATIONALISM

Fostering internationalism from an early age is important especially in children growing up in a global world. At DPS International, students absorb internationalism through the curriculum, activities, exposure to the life and works of world famous personalities, exposure to world cultures through exchange programmes etc. Country presentations by students at the morning assembly, encourage children to research the customs and cultures of different countries. In the process, they learn to see the similarities that unite people and appreciate difference as the richness of world cultures.

ENVIRONMENTAL CONSCIOUSNESS

The Eco Clubs of the senior and junior school are actively involved in raising awareness of the student and parent body. The importance of adopting eco friendly practices, the urgency of adopting next generation technologies and making a life style change is stressed. The school observes the Earth Hour, encourages recycling and promotes awareness on climate change through blogs and student networks.

SPECIAL ENGLISH LANGUAGE CLASSES

The junior school has a special ESL programme which focuses on teaching English to Economically weaker section & Disadvantaged Group and Foreign Nationals whose native/primary language is not English. In these classes every opportunity is utilized to allow learning and to practicing composite communicative language skills.
