ACADEMIC PLAN FOR GCE 'A' LEVELS

(GRADES XI & XII)

GCE 'A' Levels are one of the most highly respected qualifications around the world. They are accepted as proof of academic ability for entry into universities, institutions of higher education around the world and in the Indian sub-continent.

The subject content of each of the GCE 'A' Level syllabus has been divided into two parts.
The GCE 'AS' Level syllabus is covered in Grade XI , and the GCE 'A' Level syllabus is covered in Grade XII.

The GCE Advanced Subsidiary level (AS Level) is taken at the end of Grade A1 (XI) and the GCE Advanced level ( GCE 'A' Level) is taken at the end of Grade A2 (XII).
