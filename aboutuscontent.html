Inception:

DPS International is a core school of the DPS Society. It was launched in the year 2003 to meet the demand for International education of Indian citizens, foreign diplomats, expatriates, and NRI's. The school has its junior branch at R.K. Puram. The school boasts of a strength of over 900 students and more than 20 different nationalities. It offers personalized education with a child friendly atmosphere and a class size of 20 - 25 students. Web based research, group study and collaborative learning are the hallmarks of education at DPS International .
