Model United Nations Programme

The Model United Nations society of DPS International is run entirely by students. Over the years, the delegates from this society have gone to various Model United Nations within the city and country and won accolades for the school. Each student in this society is self-assured, has sharp insight of the current dynamics of the world and has enhanced debating skills.

Wholly managed by the student community, the Model United Nations community also teaches students more responsibility, more accountability, and more adaptability.
